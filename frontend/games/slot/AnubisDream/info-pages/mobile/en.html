Tumble Feature
This is a feature that occurs after each spin, where winning combinations are paid and winning symbols (except SCATTER symbols) disappear.
The remaining symbols fall to the bottom of the reel and empty positions are filled with new symbols falling from above.
This process continues until no new winning combinations are formed.
After all tumbles in the spin are completed, winnings are added to the player's balance.

Bonus Game Information
At least 3 SCATTER symbols trigger the Free Spins round. The Free Spins round starts with 10 spins.
During free spins, each SCATTER symbol adds +1 free spin to the spin counter.
Multiplier Reel
During the bonus game, after each winning spin, the multiplier reel spins and reveals a multiplier value.
The win obtained at the end of the spin is multiplied by this value from the multiplier reel.
Possible multiplier values are: 2x, 3x, 5x, 10x, 20x, 50x and 100x.
Buy Free Spins
The Bonus Game round can be triggered directly by purchasing.
Pay 100 times the total bet amount to trigger the Free Spins feature. In this case, at least 3 Scatter symbols are guaranteed.

Theoretical RTP
RTP (Return to Player) means the percentage return to player.
This game's overall theoretical RTP is 96.53%.
When the "Buy Feature" is active, RTP is 96.65%.
